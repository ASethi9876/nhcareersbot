# Business Administration (L3)

## Overview
As a Business Administration apprentice, you’ll connect with different parts of National Highways as a first point of contact and build relationships with our internal and external customers. 
You'll work closely with our Business Administration teams and take on tasks that keep our business running smoothly.

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You enjoy managing information, documents and records
You enjoy supporting with and organising events
You're good at engaging with people via emails and calls
You're interested in service delivery and building relationships with customers, internally and externally.
