# Customer Service Specialist (L3)

## Overview
Our Customer Service Apprentices are at the heart of our customer experience.
You’ll be the first point of contact for our customers, handling a wide range of queries. 
At our busy National Traffic Operations Centre in Birmingham, our team respond to approximately 40,000 enquiries per month! 

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You want to start a rewarding career in a customer service role
You enjoy helping customers and providing the information they need in an effective and timely way.
You're interested in supporting with projects, initiatives and public campaigns

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)
