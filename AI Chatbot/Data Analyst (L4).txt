# Data Analyst (L4)

## Overview
Our Data Analyst apprentices work closely with stakeholders across National Highways to identify their data needs, extract information and present clear, meaningful findings to a variety of audiences.
As part of the apprenticeship, you'll learn how we gather, study and present data to help inform key business decisions and projects at National Highways.

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You love collecting, organising and analysing data.
You enjoy data gathering from different sources
You enjoy communicating data and findings through technical notes, reports and presentations 
You love developing good working relationships 

## Entry Requirements:
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above) and 
2 A Levels OR equivalent Level 3 qualifications (e.g. BTEC, T Level) in a relevant subject
