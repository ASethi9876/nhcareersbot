# Data Scientist (L6)

## Overview
Our Apprentice Data Scientists tackle a range of real-world problems and understand how data science can help with solutions. 
You'll work with experienced specialists in our Digital Lab Data Science team, you'll understand and work with cloud infrastructure and explore the different stages of our data science projects. 

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You'd like to deliver digital solutions which improve and innovate our roads.
You'd love to be involved with exciting and innovative digital projects that will make a real difference on our road network.
You'd like to complete a degree in Data Science as part of your apprenticeship.

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need at least 5 GCSEs at grade 4 / grade C or above, including English and Maths, plus one of the following:        
A minimum of 104 UCAS points with A-Levels (or a mix of A-Levels and equivalent qualifications) with at least one STEM subject (Science, Technology, Engineering or Maths)
OR
An equivalent Level 3 qualification (e.g. BTEC Extended Diploma) in a related subject
OR
Completed a Level 3 apprenticeship in a related field
