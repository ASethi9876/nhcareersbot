# Procurement (L4)

## Overview
As a Procurement Apprentice, you’ll work with our procurement teams to ensure National Highways chooses the best suppliers to deliver what we need and that we get the best value for money.
Procurement is much more than just buying things!  You'll also develop relationships with suppliers and monitor supplier performance.

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You want to experience some of the largest public sector procurement activities in the country.
You enjoy considering factors such as sustainability, diversity and ethical sourcing.
You're keen to develop your business and commercial knowledge.

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)
