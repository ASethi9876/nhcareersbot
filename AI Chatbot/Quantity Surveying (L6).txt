# Quantity Surveying (L6)

## Overview:
On our Chartered Quantity Surveyor apprenticeship, you’ll work alongside our Commercial and Procurement teams to  manage the cost aspects of our complex infrastructure improvement and investment projects.
You’ll learn a range of quantity surveying techniques including forecasting, estimation, value management and whole life costing, risk management, and supply chain management to achieve best quality and value.

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You'd like to be at the centre of multi-million pound projects that connect the country.
You're interested in how contracts work.
You'd like to learn how we select specialist contractors for our projects, and how we monitor supplier performance.

## To be considered, you'll need: 
Minimum of five GCSEs (including English and maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)
And one of the following:
112 UCAS points (grades A*A* or BBC in A Levels -excludes general studies)
BTEC Extended Diploma – grades DMM in related field
A grade of merit or above in a HNC, HND or Level 3 Apprenticeship in a construction or property related discipline
