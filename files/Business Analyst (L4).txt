# Business Analyst (L4)

## Overview
Our Business Analyst Apprentices support National Highways to develop and deliver solutions for a variety of real-world business problems.
Business Analysts connect with stakeholders across the business to understand their needs and help translate these into solutions and improvements. This could be a new piece of technology, a software or application solution, or a change to ways of working. 

As an apprentice, you will gain valuable experience on exciting and innovative IT and digital projects, as well as:
Investigation techniques
Business process and data modelling
Stakeholder analysis and management
IT Service Level agreements and standard operating procedures 

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You enjoy working in a team
Are punctual 
Can communicate effectively when writing and speaking

## You’ll also need to have these essential qualifications:
Minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)
Plus, ONE of the following:
A Level 3 qualification (e.g. BTEC, A Level, T Level) in a related subject
Completed a Level 3 Apprenticeship in a related subject
