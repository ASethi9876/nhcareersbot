# Civil Engineer (L6)

## Overview
As an Apprentice Civil Engineering Technician, you'll be involved with a range of exciting projects, supporting engineers and construction professionals in one of our largest regions. 
You'll learn how we manage, monitor and maintain our network of highways assets, including 1887 miles of road! 

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You'd like to be at the centre of one of the most advanced road networks in the world.
You'd like to learn how we inspect our assets and analyse asset data
You're keen to be part of how we plan our vision for the road network

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)
