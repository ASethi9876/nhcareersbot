# Digital Product Manager (L4)

## Overview
In this customer focused role, you'll help develop, deliver and improve the digital and IT products we rely on at National Highways. 
Working with experienced specialists in our Service Operations team, you’ll get involved with a range of digital projects to deliver requirements on time and on budget. 

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above) And  A Level 3 Qualification (e.g. A Level, T Level, BTEC), or completed a Level 3 apprenticeship in any subject and Equivalent work experience in a relevant role

This apprenticeship is for you if:
You're interested in working with innovative and customer focused products.
You're keen to learn about product development and research.
You enjoy supporting customers and project teams.
You love thinking about ways
