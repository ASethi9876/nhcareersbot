# Human Resources (L5)

## Overview
Our HR apprenticeship gives you the opportunity to experience a large HR function within an evolving organisation.
You'll support our people managers with queries, manage a range of people projects and provide administrative support to key team members. 

## This apprenticeship is for you if:
You enjoy supporting people to be the best they can be.
You love building positive relationships and communicating information effectively.
You are passionate about people.

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above) And Be educated to Level 3 (e.g. BTEC, A Level or equivalent)
