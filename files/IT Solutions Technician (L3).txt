# IT Solutions Technician (L3)
## Overview
As an apprentice IT Solutions Technician, you’ll help deliver, monitor and maintain the operational technologies and systems that keep our roads running safely and smoothly.

## Entry Requirement
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above)

## This apprenticeship is for you if :
You love learning about IT Solutions and Cyber Security
You are interested in the digital architecture that sits ‘behind the scenes’ in our control rooms.
You're able to work within a team and write concisely for non- technical audiences.
