# Project Management (L4)

## Overview
As a Project Management apprentice, you'll work on exciting projects that shape how we build and operate our roads.
You'll learn about the project delivery lifecycle, planning and management. 

## Entry Requirements
To be considered, you'll need a minimum of five GCSEs (including English and Maths, at grade C/grade 4 or above) Plus, ONE of the following: At least 48 UCAS tariff points or equivalent level 3 qualifications (e.g. BTEC, T Level, NVQ or Equivalent relevant experience

## This apprenticeship is for you if :
You're interested in working on multi-million pound projects or investment schemes that have real impact for road users. 
You want to be guided by experienced project professionals to deliver successful projects.
You'd like to gain professional recognition as a member of the Association of Project Management (APM).
